Triceratops

A medium sized Herbivore that has three huge horns used to pummel it's opponents and protect it's kind

Class:ceratopsian

Diet: Leaves, Shrubs

Weight: 6,000 to 12,000 kg

Height: 2.9 to 3 meters

Speed: 32 km/h

Information

Triceratops (meaning'three-horned face') is a genus of ceratopsian dinosaur that lived during the late Cretaceous period, about 68 to 66 million years ago in what is now western North America. It was one of the last-known non-avian dinosaurs and lived until the Cretaceous extinction event 66 million years ago.
